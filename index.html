Case Study

Improving dashboard performance by moving logic closer to the data.

A simple example of how I think through BI problems: diagnose the bottleneck, simplify the model, and make the reporting experience faster for users.

Problem

Power BI dashboards were slowed down by heavy DAX calculations and refresh logic, creating delays for users who needed daily operational visibility.

Approach

Reviewed report logic, identified calculations better handled in SQL, and shifted complex transformations into pre-aggregated SQL views.

Result

Reduced daily dashboard load time by 10 minutes and improved refresh performance by 90%, creating a faster and more scalable reporting experience.
